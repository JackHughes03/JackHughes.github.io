Jailbreaking:

According to KasperSky:

"Jailbreaking allows the device owner to gain full access to the root of the operating system and access all the features."

I didn't like how I couldn't change what I wanted on my iPhone, I wanted more.
I came across jailbreaking and jailbroke my first device (iPod touch 4th gen).
From then on I've just been solififying and expanding my knowledge of iPhones and computers in general.
In 2019 during the first lockdown...I started to get tired of just using the jailbreak and addons that come with it so I decided to look into icon creating.
The first time I deployed my own 'theme' onto my iDevice I was rushed with an excitement and freedom.
Although this wasn't enough, I wanted more.
I then looked into creating the 'tweaks', also known as 'addons' and realised that they required Objective-C code.
During the next year or so, I released a series paid and free 'tweaks' in a developer name.
Although my Objective-C still isn't great, I learnt so many important lessons:
